## Description  *(max 4000)*
```
La Berceuse is a sleep instrument — a lullaby for the racing mind.

It is built for the real night: in the dark, one-handed, screen dimmed,
someone perhaps asleep beside you. One tap to start the night, sounds that
fade themselves out, and nothing that asks for your attention.

Everything happens on your device. No account, no network, no tracking.

THE BREATH
• A warm orb that breathes with you: 4-7-8, box breathing, coherent 5.5,
  the physiological sigh.
• The phase word, the count, a gentle haptic in rhythm with the breath.

THE COGNITIVE SHUFFLE
• A recognized sleep-onset technique: a slow stream of unrelated, neutral
  words, shown large and softly spoken, to unhook the mind from its loops.

THE SOUNDS
• Soundscapes synthesized live — rain, wind, waves, brown or pink noise, a
  warm drone, a music box. Nothing is a loop: the rain drifts between drizzle
  and downpour, the wind gusts, every wave is new.
• Mix them, balance them, keep your favourite blends.

THE GENERATIVE MUSIC
• Music that never ends, composed on your device: slow pads, a gliding bass,
  a harp-like melody resting in its own echo.
• It follows the hour of the night, locks onto your breathing and — if you
  allow it — softens with your heart rate (Health, on-device only).
• Three programs — Sleep, Unwind, Breathe — and fine controls: intensity,
  complexity, pulse, echo, reverb.

DEEP REST
• Guided NSDR / yoga-nidra sessions (body scan, progressive relaxation),
  revealed line by line, with an optional soft voice.
• The music keeps playing underneath; the sleep timer governs everything.

RITUALS AND THE “SLEEP” BUTTON
• Chain the instruments into one-tap sequences: “breath 3 min → deep rest →
  sounds with a timer”.
• A ritual can become the “Dors” button: one tap and the night begins.
• “Hey Siri, good night” opens straight into your ritual.

THE NIGHT ITSELF
• A sleep timer (15 to 90 min, or custom) fades everything to silence, then
  truly stops.
• A quiet countdown on the Lock Screen (Live Activity).
• Nightstand mode: a faint clock, the night sky, nothing else.
• At 3 a.m., the app offers one large button — two minutes of sighs, then the
  shuffle — not a menu.

THE NIGHT JOURNAL
• With your permission, the app reads your sleep from Health and sets it
  beside your rituals: “On ritual nights, you sleep 32 minutes longer.”
• Observations, never a dashboard.

CALM BY DESIGN
• Entirely offline: no account, no ads, no analytics, no data transmitted.
• True black, deeply dimmable, made for OLED screens at night.
• Respects Reduce Motion.
• Bilingual: French (Québécois) and English.
• Audio keeps playing under the Lock Screen.
```
